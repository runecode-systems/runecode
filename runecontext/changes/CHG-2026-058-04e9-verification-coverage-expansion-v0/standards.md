## Resolution Notes
This feature expands what RuneCode must capture and verify about authority, policy, runtime, approvals, provider use, egress, anchoring, degraded posture, and verifier identity.

The selected standards keep that work aligned with:

- canonical control-plane contracts and fail-closed policy reasoning
- reviewed approval and verifier identity binding
- least-privilege secret and provider access semantics
- explicit runtime-evidence posture and audit-verification rules
- one local-first architecture that records denials, missing evidence, and degraded trust claims instead of hiding them
